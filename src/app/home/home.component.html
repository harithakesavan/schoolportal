OUR SPECIALITIES


LAB FACILITIES


The school has distinct and separate science laboratories for practical sessions in Physics, Chemistry, Home Science, and Biology in accordance with the CBSE curriculum. The laboratories are spacious and offer modern and excellent equipment where our students are encouraged to use their analytical and creative skills through research and experimentation. Apart from the Middle and Secondary School, students of the Primary School also have regular laboratory sessions in their curriculum.

CANTEEN FACILITIES


School canteen facility aims to provide children of working parents to buy nutritious and healthy food at competitive prices during the school day. Meals are either cooked on the premises or bought from approved restaurants and food outlets. Pupils who cannot take full breakfast from home may bring some form of healthy ‘snack’ with them for break time. Pupils can also buy snacks items and fruits from the snacks counter.
